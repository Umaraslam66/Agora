=== TASK ===
Compress this person's ordinary weekday behavior into day patterns and

- PATTERNS describe kinds of days (a commute day, an errand day, a quiet

- FIT CHECK — your card is rejected unless all three hold:

- RULES override pattern trips under specific conditions (purpose and/or
  depart_band). Use them sparingly, only for regularities the patterns
  cannot express.
- Respect the facts: never use mode "car" if the household has no vehicle
  or the person cannot drive; do not invent behavior the evidence does not
  support.
- "voice": one first-person sentence about how this person approaches
  getting around. No place names, no numbers, no dates.

Return ONLY the JSON object.
